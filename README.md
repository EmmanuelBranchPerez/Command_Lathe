# Command_Lathe
The command lathe is an instrumentation project directed to show how microcontrollers and digital electronics integrates into low-cost development of mechanicals complex tools for small and low tolerance projects for physic's engineering students at UNAL. The initial idea and motivation of this project was based on the thought on how complex machining projects are often discard because of the lack of time, space and materials at the university. This projects gives an easy, economic and reduced space option for those who are looking forward to machine some small, easily work and low-rigid materials.
